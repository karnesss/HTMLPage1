This course is open to all skill levels, but we recommend a basic understanding of beginner art concepts to get the most out of the course.

Yes! You will have lifetime access to all the course material in the account that you purchased the course with.

Ross designed the syllabus around the content he felt most essential and helpful from his art school experience. The curriculum is a foundational crash course covering a wide range of subjects including: character design, environments, portraits, master studies, and more.

Although Ross uses Photoshop in the course videos, we invite you to use your preferred program of choice. The course material is not software specific, and is to be applied broadly to all forms of digital art.
