About ROSSDRAWS

Ross Tran is an illustrator and concept artist that has been in the art industry for over 10 years. He loved making up characters and worlds as a kid, which is something that hasn't changed much over the years. He began pursuing art more seriously at 16 and moved from San Jose to Los Angeles a year later to pursue a degree in Industrial Design at Art Center.

By 19 he worked as a character designer on his first feature film, Earth to Echo. A while after, he took a break to explore other interests and try his hand at acting, only to find a home on YouTube with his channel RossDraws -- where he makes lots of art ✨

He recently landed on the Forbes 30 under 30 list and is planning to turn his first graphic novel NIMA into an animated series. The book is now available on Amazon

He also loves his dog Milo, but everyone knows that.
